🏥 MediCare – Your Trusted Health Partner
MediCare is an online healthcare platform that connects customers with lab tests, pharmacies, and health services – all from the comfort of their homes. Built with a user‑friendly interface and powered by modern web technologies, MediCare aims to make healthcare accessible, affordable, and hassle‑free.

🌟 Key Features
🔬 Lab Tests at Home
Schedule a wide range of diagnostic tests (CBC, LFT, Lipid Profile, Thyroid, Full Body Checkup, etc.). Samples are collected by professional phlebotomists from your doorstep.

💊 Nearby Pharmacies
Locate nearby pharmacies and order medicines online. The platform uses IP‑based city detection to show you relevant shops in your area.

🔐 Secure Authentication
Firebase handles user login, signup, and session management – ensuring your data stays safe.

📦 Real‑Time Order Tracking
Customers can track their lab test bookings and medicine deliveries in real time.

👨‍⚕️ Partner Dashboard
Retailers and lab partners get a dedicated dashboard to manage orders, update inventory, and view analytics.

✅ OTP Sample Collection
For lab tests, a one‑time password (OTP) system ensures that the sample is collected from the correct person – adding an extra layer of security and trust.

🛠️ Technology Stack
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (vanilla + Firebase SDK)

Backend & Auth: Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Hosting)

Hosting: InfinityFree (free PHP/MySQL hosting, used for static files and optional PHP scripts)

Additional Tools: FontAwesome, Google Fonts, custom CSS variables

🎯 Mission
To bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers by offering a seamless digital platform that prioritises convenience, transparency, and reliability
